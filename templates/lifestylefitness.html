🥗 My Lifestyle & Fitness Philosophy 🏋️‍♂️

I’m an early bird who enjoys starting the day around 6 AM and winding down by 11 PM. My daily routine reflects a deep commitment to healthy living, especially through mindful eating and physical activity.

  • 🥣 Breakfast: My signature meal includes oats, milk, assorted nuts, and bananas — oil-free and nutrient-dense.
  • 🥗 Lunch: A wholesome and filling meal (mostly Indian) with minimal oil.
  • 🍇 Dinner: A light and refreshing plate of seasonal fruits, boiled eggs, and a small portion of carbohydrates, always cooked without oil.

I usually cook my own meals and rarely order from outside, allowing me to maintain full control over the ingredients and nutritional quality of my food.

While I used to go to the gym daily, I’ve recently shifted toward more enjoyable activities like walking 🚶‍♂️ and bicycling 🚴‍♀️. That said, I may return to the gym soon as part of my evolving fitness journey.
